Help

Basics

The aim of the game is to write and publish The Unabridged History of Everything .

For this you need words. To make words, you need letters.

Make letters by pressing the big button. (To help you, vowels are more likely to be made :D)

Use those letters to make words by typing in the textbox under the box where your letters go. You can see how many words you have on the "Current" tab.

Use words to publish stuff. Start by publishing poems!

Publishing stuff earns you royalties. Upgrade your stuff to earn more royalties.

Spend your royalties on equipment or other stuff (see "Equipment" tab and "Purchase" tab on the right. More upgrades coming soon).

Equipment

There are many types of equipment. For most you can upgrade the rate (which increases the amount of words/letters earned) and the tick-rate (increases how often the equipment does its thing). Equipments that make words are grouped to have the same tickrate.

The random word finders will pick a random word from your dictionary (see "Stats" for list of words in your dictionary) and try make that word from the letters in your box.

The specific word finders will look for a word you specify. To pick a word, press the specific word finder in the equipment tab (literally click the name of the equipment). A thing should pop up.
